which means the orchestration decision is made by the agent, not the user — making the audit trail not optional, but the primary mechanism by which users understand what happened on their behalf.

---

The week's macro signal, across all six products: **the trust surface is expanding to match the autonomy surface.** Every new grant of delegation — Goal Mode hours-long runs, dynamic workflows across hundreds of agents, always-on Personal Computer, cross-app Connectors — is now landing with an adjacent control mechanism: an audit trail, a kill switch, an attribution indicator, a mid-flight step schema, an explicit approval gate. The industry has not yet built any of these to full fidelity, but for the first time, all six major platforms are building them *simultaneously*, in the same release window, in direct response to the same user anxiety: *if I let the agent work, how do I know what it did?*
