, including a `terminalSequence` hook field that lets agents emit desktop notifications and window titles without a controlling terminal, and a `/feedback` flow that can now include sessions from the last 24 hours or 7 days for issues spanning more than a single session. Both companies are landing on the same insight: as agents become persistent, users need named primitives — Skills, Hooks, Sessions — that they can inspect, name, share, and revoke. The "prompt" is no longer the unit of work; the workflow is.
