— xAI is building a two-tier agentic UX: Skills for recurring structured tasks, Imagine for open-ended creative delegation. The design challenge both Perplexity and xAI now face is the same: as agents gain persistent memory and scheduled execution, the "undo" and "review" primitives become the most important UX surface in the product.
