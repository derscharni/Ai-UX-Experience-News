## Product Highlights

### The Infrastructure of Agentic Commerce

While OpenAI recently pulled back on its Instant Checkout feature, it hasn't abandoned commerce. Instead, it's building the infrastructure. **OpenAI has introduced the Agentic Commerce Protocol (ACP)**, a new open standard designed to facilitate AI-driven transactions [2]. This suggests a strategic pivot from building a proprietary checkout experience to creating the underlying plumbing that allows agents to interact with any storefront. Concurrently, ChatGPT's shopping capabilities have been enhanced with visual discovery tools and direct integration with Shopify storefronts [1], indicating a more visual and integrated approach to agentic shopping.

### The Agent Goes Mobile and Multi-Platform

The push to make agents accessible everywhere continues. **Anthropic has significantly upgraded the Claude mobile app**, enabling it to handle complex work tasks without needing a laptop [4]. By integrating with tools like Figma, Canva, and Amplitude, the mobile app is becoming a powerful hub for managing workflows on the go. This aligns with the broader trend of moving away from the desktop as the sole locus of agentic work.

Similarly, **Google is refining the Gemini experience across devices**. A cleaner, unified interface with a redesigned sidebar is rolling out to Android users, and Gemini features are being integrated into Google TV [6]. For developers, the integration of Gemini 3 Pro into the Gemini CLI brings state-of-the-art reasoning and agentic coding directly into their terminal workflows [5].

### The UX of Trust and Safety

As agents become more capable, the UX of trust and safety remains a critical challenge. **xAI is facing a lawsuit from the city of Baltimore** concerning Grok's "spicy mode" and its generation of explicit content [10]. This highlights the ongoing tension between providing unrestricted AI capabilities and ensuring user safety and content moderation. The way platforms handle these issues—through UI warnings, content filters, and clear user controls—will be a defining factor in the long-term success of agentic products.
