# UX Briefing: Desktop Deepening and Cross-Platform Integration

**April 30, 2026**

Good morning. Today's briefing wraps up April with a clear trend: AI agents are embedding themselves deeper into specialized environments, from 3D modeling software and iPad multitasking to automotive dashboards and local file systems.

## At a Glance

| Product | Key Development |
| --- | --- |
| **Claude** | Anthropic launched Claude Design Connectors for SketchUp and Adobe, enabling users to generate and modify 3D geometry and designs through conversational prompts. [1] |
| **Manus AI** | Integrated OpenAI's GPT Image 2 model into Manus Slides for 4K photorealistic generation, and expanded its Google Workspace capabilities to read, write, and organize Drive files directly. [2] |
| **Google Gemini** | GM is replacing Google Assistant with Gemini across 4 million vehicles, shifting the in-car UX from simple voice commands to complex, multimodal AI interactions. [3] |
| **Perplexity** | Redesigned its iPad app with a native interface, multitasking support, and desktop-style navigation, optimizing the "Comet" browser for tablet workflows. [4] |
| **Microsoft Copilot** | Rolled out smarter editing in Excel with multi-step plans and Python integration, while simultaneously removing Copilot buttons from core Windows 11 apps like Notepad. [5] |
| **Grok (xAI)** | Expanded its multimodal suite with new Text-to-Speech voices and real-time transcription for voice call streaming, alongside a new low-cost subscription tier. [6] |

## Product Highlights

### Agents in the 3D Workspace

Anthropic is aggressively moving Claude beyond text and 2D UI. The launch of Claude Design Connectors for SketchUp and Adobe introduces a new interaction pattern for 3D modeling. [1] Instead of manually manipulating vertices and textures, designers can now describe 3D geometry and brainstorm spatial concepts conversationally, with Claude generating the corresponding models directly within the SketchUp environment. This significantly lowers the technical barrier for 3D design and accelerates the prototyping phase.

### The In-Car AI UX Battle

The dashboard is becoming the next major battleground for AI agents. Following reports of xAI testing Grok in Tesla vehicles, Google announced it is integrating Gemini into 4 million GM vehicles, effectively replacing the legacy Google Assistant. [3] This transition represents a massive shift in automotive UX: moving from rigid, command-based interactions ("turn on the AC," "navigate home") to fluid, conversational, and context-aware assistance that can handle complex, multi-turn queries while driving.

### Perplexity Optimizes for the Tablet

While much of the recent focus has been on desktop and mobile, Perplexity is addressing the tablet UX. The company redesigned its iPad app to feature a truly native interface, complete with multitasking support and desktop-style navigation. [4] This optimizes the "Comet" AI-native browser for power users who rely on iPads for research and productivity, allowing them to run Perplexity Computer's multi-step workflows seamlessly alongside other applications.
